M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics) Program in Tamil Nadu

Cheran College of Pharmacy in Coimbatore offers a top-quality M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics) program in Tamil Nadu, designed for students who have completed a Bachelor of Pharmacy. Our program focuses on modern learning, hands-on research, and real-world pharmaceutical practice.
We aim to prepare students for exciting careers in the fast-growing healthcare and drug development industries, making us one of the preferred MPharm colleges in Tamil Nadu.

What is M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics)?

The M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics) course is a 2-year postgraduate program (4 semesters), which includes detailed study and a research thesis. Students explore key topics like:

  • Drug Regulatory Affairs
  • Pharmacokinetics
  • Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Technology
  • Drug Analysis and Testing

FAQ

  1. What is the eligibility for M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics)?
    A B.Pharm degree with 50% marks from a recognized university. Must be a registered pharmacist.
  2. What is the course duration and structure?
    It’s a 2-year full-time program with theory, practicals, and a final research project.
  3. What are the career options after M.Pharm?
    You can work in R&D, pharma companies, regulatory affairs, QA/QC, or pursue Ph.D. and teaching.
  4. Does Cheran College offer placements for M.Pharm?
    Yes, with training, mock interviews, and tie-ups with top pharma companies.
  5. Are hostel and transport facilities available?
    Yes, separate hostels for boys and girls, hygienic food, Wi-Fi, and safe college bus service.

Who Can Apply?

  • You need a Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) degree from a PCI/AICTE-approved college or university.
  • A minimum of 50% marks in your undergraduate course is required.
  • This program is perfect for those who want to go deeper into the field of drug formulation, development, and safety.

Core Subjects in M.Pharm Pharmaceutics

At Cheran College of Pharmacy, our M.Pharm program in Tamil Nadu gives you access to top-quality labs, experienced faculty and a student-focused learning system.

Key Subjects You’ll Study:

  • Organic Chemistry
  • Modern Analytical Techniques
  • Pharmaceutical Technology
  • Modern Pharmaceutics
  • Industrial Pharmacy
  • Pharmacokinetics and Biopharmaceutics
  • Drug Analysis Techniques
  • Pathophysiology

We make sure students gain advanced knowledge, hands-on training and research experience to excel in the pharmaceutical field.

Career Opportunities After M.Pharm in Tamil Nadu

Graduates from MPharm colleges in Tamil Nadu are in high demand across industries. Our students have gone on to build strong careers in pharmaceutical companies, hospitals, labs, and government roles.
