About Cheran College of Pharmacy

Guiding Future Pharmacists to Excellence

Cheran College of Pharmacy (CCOP), established in 1992, holds the proud distinction of being the first pharmacy college in Tamil Nadu. Recognized by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), the college offers B.Pharm, Pharm.D and M.Pharm - Pharmaceutics, M.Pharm - Pharmacology, and M.Pharm - Pharmaceutical Analysis, making it one of the most reputed Pharmacy Colleges in Tamil Nadu.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, leadership, and a positive societal impact through transformative education and research.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver transformative education that nurtures academic excellence, innovation, and global citizenship, while fostering community engagement and collaboration.

Our History

Established in 1992, we have over 30 years of experience in pharmacy education, delivering academic excellence and industry-ready professionals.
