Refund and Cancellation Policy

  • Home

1. Cancellation Policy

  • Once a user has successfully registered and completed payment through the online payment gateway, the transaction is considered final.
  • No cancellation requests will be entertained under any circumstances.
  • The College does not offer refunds for withdrawal from a course, non-attendance, or dissatisfaction with academic or administrative services.

2. Refund Policy

  • Refunds are permitted only in the event of excess payment due to technical errors, duplicate transactions, or other valid and verifiable reasons.
  • To request a refund, the applicant must submit a written request to the College's Administrative Office, along with relevant proof such as transaction receipts or bank statements.
  • Upon verification and approval, eligible refunds will be processed within a reasonable time frame through the original mode of payment.
  • Processing time may vary based on the payment provider or banking procedures involved.
  • The College reserves the right to reject refund claims that are incomplete, fraudulent, or unsupported by sufficient documentation.
  • All decisions made by the College with regard to refund claims are final and binding.
