1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help enhance your browsing experience and allow us to improve the functionality and performance of our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Remember your preferences and settings for future visits.
  • Monitor website traffic and analyze user behavior to enhance our services.
  • Maintain website security and verify user authentication.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytical Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website, so we can improve its structure and content.
  • Functional Cookies: These remember user preferences to provide a personalized experience.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of the website.
