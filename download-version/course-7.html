Pharm.D (Post Baccalaureate) in Tamil Nadu

Cheran College of Pharmacy offers an advanced Pharm.D (Post Baccalaureate) program designed for students who have completed their B.Pharm degree. This three-year postgraduate course is structured to enhance your clinical skills and knowledge in the pharmaceutical sciences, preparing you for leading roles in healthcare.
Pursue a future-ready Doctor of Pharmacy in Tamil Nadu and become a vital part of patient care, drug safety, and advanced pharmacy practice.

Why Choose Pharm.D in Tamil Nadu at Cheran?

Practical Learning & Internship Opportunities

Tamil Nadu is a hub for advanced healthcare and pharmacy education. At Cheran, the Pharm.D (Post Baccalaureate) program emphasizes hands-on clinical training, giving students exposure to hospital pharmacy settings, patient interaction, and real-time diagnosis and drug management.

Course Highlights

  • Postgraduate Level: Specialized program for B.Pharm graduates.
  • Industry-Relevant Curriculum: Tailored to meet the evolving demands of the healthcare and pharmaceutical industry.
  • Focus on Clinical Skills: Gain expertise in disease diagnosis, drug therapy, patient monitoring, and pharmaceutical care.

FAQ - Pharm.D (PB)

  1. What is Pharm.D (PB)?
    Pharm.D (PB) is a 3-year post-baccalaureate program for B.Pharm graduates, focusing on advanced pharmacy practice and clinical research.
  2. What are the eligibility requirements?
    Candidates need a B.Pharm degree with a minimum of 50% marks to apply. Admission is based on the entrance exam and interview.
  3. What career options are available after Pharm.D (PB)?
    Graduates can work as clinical pharmacists, pharmacy managers, researchers, or pharmaceutical consultants in hospitals and industries.
  4. How long is the Pharm.D (PB) course?
    The program lasts 3 years, combining academic study and clinical training.
  5. Is there hostel accommodation?
    Yes, separate hostels for boys and girls are available with modern facilities and faculty supervision.

Program Overview

Course Duration and Structure

The Pharm.D (PB) course at Cheran College of Pharmacy is spread across 3 years, with semester-wise examinations. The program is approved by the Pharmacy Council of India and is structured into:

  • Phase 1: Two years of intensive classroom and laboratory-based learning.
  • Phase 2: One year of internship/residency in hospitals, focusing on real-time clinical pharmacy practice under expert supervision.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have completed Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) from an institution recognized by the Pharmacy Council of India.
