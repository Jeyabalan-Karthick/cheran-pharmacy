Course Overview: M Pharm in Tamilnadu

What is M.Pharm (Pharmaceutical Analysis)?

The M.Pharm in Pharmaceutical Analysis is a two-year postgraduate course that focuses on the identification, quantification, and quality assurance of pharmaceutical substances. The program trains students in cutting-edge analytical techniques used for drug development and regulatory compliance.

Course Duration and Structure

  • Level: Postgraduate
  • Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
  • Mode of Examination: Semester-wise
  • Eligibility: B.Pharm with minimum 50% from a recognized institution

This master of pharmacy in Tamilnadu combines classroom theory, laboratory experiments, and dissertation-based research to create skilled pharmaceutical professionals.

Specialization-Oriented Subjects

  • Cosmetic and Cosmeceuticals
