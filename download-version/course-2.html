B Pharm in Tamilnadu

Cheran College of Pharmacy, located in Coimbatore, is one of the well known BPharm colleges in TamilNadu. Our college focuses on offering quality education in pharmaceutical sciences with a student-first approach. We create a vibrant learning environment that connects students with top practices in the medical, research, and healthcare industries.

B.Pharm FAQs

  1. What is the eligibility for B.Pharm at Cheran College?
    10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Math. Diploma holders can apply for lateral entry.
  2. Is Cheran College of Pharmacy PCI approved?
    Yes, it is approved by PCI and affiliated with The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University.
  3. What is the duration of the B.Pharm course?
    The B.Pharm course is 4 years (8 semesters) including practical and project work.
  4. What are the career options after B.Pharm?
    You can work as a pharmacist, drug analyst, medical coder, or pursue M.Pharm.
  5. Are hostel and transport facilities available?
    Yes, safe hostels and college bus services are available for all B.Pharm students.

Eligibility for Bachelor of Pharmacy in Tamil Nadu

To join the Bachelor of Pharmacy in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, students must meet the following eligibility criteria:

Basic Eligibility:

  • Must have passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry and Biology/Mathematics.
  • Minimum required percentage as per state regulations.

Additional Requirements:

  • Students may need to qualify in entrance exams as per Tamil Nadu pharmacy college norms.
  • Admission guidelines follow those of Dr. MGR Medical University and government authorities.
