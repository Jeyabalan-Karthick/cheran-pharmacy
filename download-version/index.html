F. A. Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about pharmacy courses, careers, and the advantages of studying at Cheran College of Pharmacy.

Cheran College of Pharmacy, Coimbatore is one of the top choices in Tamil Nadu, offering recognized B.Pharm, Pharm.D and M.Pharm programs. It combines expert faculty, modern labs and industry exposure for a strong pharmacy career.
B.Pharm is a 4 year undergraduate program, while D.Pharm (Diploma in Pharmacy) is 2 years. Pharm.D is 6 years, including practical training in hospitals and industry.
B.Pharm is ideal for advanced career opportunities, research, and higher studies. D.Pharm is shorter and focused on practical pharmacy work, mainly for hospital or retail jobs.
Top pharmacy careers include clinical pharmacist, pharmaceutical researcher, quality control analyst, and hospital pharmacist. Cheran College equips students with skills for these high-demand roles.
Pharmacy and MBBS are different fields; pharmacy focuses on drugs and healthcare solutions, while MBBS trains as a doctor. Pharmacy is suitable for those interested in research, industry, and pharmaceutical careers.
