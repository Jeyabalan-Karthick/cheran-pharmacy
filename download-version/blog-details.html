Your Complete Guide to M.Pharm Admission 2026 at Cheran College of Pharmacy

Want to take your pharmacy journey further? A Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm) in Tamil Nadu at Cheran College of Pharmacy is your key to unlocking exciting opportunities in research, development, and high-paying jobs in the pharmaceutical world.

We know the admission process can feel confusing. That’s why we’ve created this simple, step-by-step guide to walk you through everything: from eligibility to entrance exams and finally, how to apply.

Let’s get started on your journey to becoming a master of pharmacy in Tamil Nadu with Cheran College of Pharmacy.

M.Pharm Eligibility

Before you jump in, let’s make sure you have the right qualifications. The basic rules are similar for most M.Pharm colleges in Tamil Nadu:

  • Your Degree: You should hold a B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy) degree from a college recognized by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI).
  • Your Marks: You typically need a minimum of 55% to 60% marks in your B.Pharm degree. (At Cheran College, we require a minimum of 55% aggregate).
  • Your Internship: You should have completed your compulsory internship if it was part of your B.Pharm course.

M.Pharm Entrance Exams

Your score in a national or state-level entrance exam is your ticket to admission. Here are the main ones to know about:

  • GPAT (Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test): This is the big one. GPAT is a national-level exam, and a good score is accepted by almost all top M.Pharm colleges in Tamil Nadu, including ours. It checks your knowledge of pharmacy subjects.
  • TNPSC and State-Level Exams: Some seats are filled through exams conducted by the state government. Stay updated with their announcements if you’re considering this option.
  • Direct Admission Based on Marks: Don’t have an entrance exam score? Don’t worry! Many colleges, including us, offer a certain number of seats for students based directly on their B.Pharm marks. This is often called the Management Quota.

M.Pharm Specializations at Cheran

Choosing your specialty is a big part of your M.Pharm journey. It decides what you'll become a master of! At Cheran College of Pharmacy, we offer specializations that match the hottest trends in the industry:

  • 1. Pharmaceutics: Learn how to develop new drugs and turn them into safe, effective medicines like tablets, capsules, and injections.
  • 2. Pharmacology: Dive deep into how drugs work inside the body. This is perfect for those interested in research and drug discovery.
  • 3. Pharmaceutical Analysis: Become an expert in testing medicines to ensure their quality, safety, and purity.
  • 4. Pharmacy Practice: Focus on the patient-facing side of pharmacy, often working directly in hospitals to advise on the best use of medicines.

Choosing the right specialization is the key to a fulfilling career.

Your 5-Step Application Guide

Ready to apply? The process is simple. Here’s how you can secure your seat for the Master of Pharmacy in Tamil Nadu program at Cheran College:

  1. Check Your Eligibility: First, make sure you meet the criteria we mentioned above. Gather all your documents like mark sheets and your internship completion certificate.
  2. Get Your Exam Score (If applicable): If you are applying with GPAT or another exam score, make sure you have your scorecard ready.
  3. Fill the Form Online: Visit our official website and find the ‘Admissions’ section. Click on the M.Pharm application form and fill in your personal, academic, and exam details carefully.
  4. Upload Your Documents: You’ll need to upload scanned copies of your photo, ID proof, B.Pharm mark sheets, and your entrance exam scorecard (if you have one).
  5. Pay the Fee & Submit: Finally, pay the application fee securely through our online payment gateway. Once you submit, note down your application number for future reference.

That’s it! After that, our admissions team will reach out to guide you on the next steps.

Why Do Students Choose Cheran College?

With so many options for M.Pharm in Tamil Nadu, why do students pick us? It’s more than just a degree.

  • Expert Teachers: Learn from highly qualified professors who have real-world industry and research experience.
  • Top-Notch Labs: Get hands-on training in our advanced laboratories filled with modern equipment.
  • Great Placements: We have a strong track record of placing our graduates in leading pharmaceutical companies and hospitals.

We don’t just teach you from books; we prepare you for a real career.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is GPAT compulsory for M.Pharm at Cheran College?
A: No, it is not the only way. While we accept GPAT scores, we also offer direct admission based on your B.Pharm marks for eligible students.

Q: What is the last date to apply?
A: Admission deadlines can change. The best way is to check the ‘Admissions’ page on our website or call our counseling team directly for the most current information.

Q: What are the career options after an M.Pharm?
A: An M.Pharm degree opens doors to become a Research Scientist, Medical Coder, Clinical Researcher, Professor, Regulatory Manager, and much more in the multi-billion dollar pharma industry.
